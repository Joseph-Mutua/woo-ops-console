# Sample Use Cases

## 1. Payment recovery queue

A store operator filters the dashboard to `Failed payment`, selects the affected orders, adds a note for support, and exports the list to finance for gateway follow-up.

## 2. Fulfillment delay sweep

The ops lead filters to `Long-unfulfilled order`, scans risk scores, and marks the most urgent orders for warehouse review before customer escalations increase.

## 3. Refund anomaly review

A merchant sees a spike in refund anomalies, opens the flagged orders, and uses the AI-assisted explanation to understand the likely operational root cause before making policy changes.

## 4. Missing shipping detail cleanup

Support filters to `Missing shipping details`, adds internal notes for orders missing destination fields, then contacts only the customers who truly need clarification.
